Mad for Fun

The Monster range grows with the SP version, designed to enhance fun, thanks to technical equipment perfect for those who love sporty riding and embellish style with the Moto GP-inspired livery.

The Monster SP takes the 2022 colors of the official team's Desmosedici GP, mounts Öhlins suspensions and Termignoni homologated silencer, Brembo Stylema® calipers and Pirelli Diablo Rosso IV tires. True sporty equipment that will make you fall in love with the new Monster SP, available also in depowered version.
